1. Get a license key

To get a PRO license for yourself click here: GET A LICENSE
If you want to get a bunch of promo codes for your devmins, contact the support.
If you have any trouble or questions have a look at the FAQ page where you can find all the info needed to contact the ORGanizer support.
Remember that you can change the email address linked to your purchased license using the Manage Membership link at the bottom of the Gumroad email address or by contacting the support.

2. Validate your license

Copy the license key you have purchased in the following input text and click the Validate & Apply button to validate it.
To check your new limits, jump to the Quota tab.
Upon a successfull license verification, you'll be required to activate this client: an email will be sent to your registered email address containing a verificaton code that you must input into the "Activation code" textbox that will eventually show up.
N.B. If you reached the active clients limit for your license type (this feature will be activated in the next weeks), you'll be required to deactivate a client from the list that will pop up after the license verification process.
Client deactivation frequency has a cooldown that depends on the license type (e.g. if you just activated a client you'll not enabled to deactivate it immediatly, but you'll need to wait the cooldown time, whose amount depends on license type).
Sharing license key and email is forbidden and leads to immediate license expiration with no refund.
